Our Story

Born on the Streets
of Bangkok

Siam Street was born from a single obsession: the incredible, chaos-of-flavour street food that fills the alleys of Bangkok at midnight. Our founder, Chef Niran Chaiyaporn, grew up navigating those alleys — watching vendors turn a wok of smoke and heat into something transcendent.

After training in both Bangkok and Paris, Niran returned with one mission — to carry those flavours faithfully into a modern dining experience. Every dish on our menu is tested against his memory of those streets: the tang of tamarind, the perfume of holy basil, the slow burn of fresh chilli.

We source our herbs and spices from small growers in Chiang Rai and import our nam pla, kapi shrimp paste, and specialty chillis directly from Thailand. Nothing is compromised. Everything is earned.

  • 🌱
    Seasonal & Local Produce Partnered with regional farms for the freshest seasonal ingredients.
  • ✈️
    Imported Authenticity Key pastes, sauces, and dried spices air-freighted from Thailand monthly.
  • ♻️
    Zero Waste Kitchen Compost-first, plastic-free, and committed to sustainable hospitality.
